About The Gardener's World

Founded in 2008, The Gardener's World has grown from a small family business to Kampala's premier landscaping company. Our passion for creating beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces has helped transform hundreds of properties across the city.

We believe in working with nature, not against it. Our team of skilled professionals combines traditional gardening knowledge with modern techniques to deliver exceptional results that stand the test of time.

Eco-friendly and sustainable practices
